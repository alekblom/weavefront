Weavefront

Deploy to the decentralized web from one panel

Open source hosting panel for Web3. Deploy static sites and dApps to IPFS, Arweave, and Filecoin. Manage domains, storage, and deployments with AI-assisted tooling.

◎

Multi-Protocol Deploy

Push to IPFS, Arweave, or Filecoin from one interface. Git-based workflows or direct upload.

⚛

Domain Management

Connect traditional domains, ENS names, and Handshake domains. SSL and gateway config included.

⍑

AI Assistant

Natural language deployment, diagnostics, and optimization. Ask it to deploy, debug, or configure.

⌂

Self-Hosted

Run on your own server. Your infrastructure, your keys, your responsibility. Fully open source.

⚖

Storage Dashboard

Monitor pinning, permanent storage costs, and replication across protocols from one view.

⚙

Rust Backend

Built in Rust for performance and safety. Lightweight, fast, and reliable on minimal hardware.
